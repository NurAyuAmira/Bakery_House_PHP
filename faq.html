More Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You may place an order through our website; by calling us at (+60)123821141; or by visiting our bakery. Photographs may be e-mailed to us at yuz9690@gmail.com.

What if I don't like what I purchased?

We rarely hear complaints about our products, which is why we've stuck around for 40 years. If you have concerns about a purchase, please kindly return the product to us on the next business day following your purchase. We do not offer refunds on returned products but may work with you on a store credit or product exchange.

What payment method can I use?

For payment on Bakery House, we accept online banking. After that, you have to upload the transfer receipt on the checkout page.

How to make an order in website?

  1. You can choose the food you want on the menu and click the order button to add it to the cart.
  2. After that, you can go to the cart page to check your order and click the checkout button.
  3. In the checkout page, you will have to complete the required details and upload your online banking receipt and click the submit button.
  4. Then your order will be placed.
